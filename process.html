Before the Tub Refinishing Starts

Our company uses only top quality products and modern tools to deliver outstanding customer service to everyone in Charlotte, NC. Our well-trained technicians are also certified and insured. Each one has years of experience reglazing everything from bathtubs and sinks to cabinets and countertops. When it comes to refinishing, reglazing and resurfacing, we are the absolute best in the Charlotte, NC.

1

We start by removing the pain from your old tub using various products (depending on the material of the tub).*

2

The tub is cleaned and all the loose caulking is removed.

3

The surface of the tub is sanded down and all the dust and debris is thoroughly cleaned.

4

The tub is then washed to make sure the surface is 100% clean.

5

The chips (if any) are repaired. In case there are signs of rust, they are carefully removed.

6

The tub is carefully wiped to prepare it for the refinishing procedure.

*In case you have previously refinished the tub, please let our expert know of this. For an additional fee, we can remove the old glaze for better results. The alternative is to sand down the tub and apply the new glaze over the old one.

Masking the Room and Other Fixtures

Masking paper is used to cover the area surrounding the fixture.

All the other fixtures that should not be reglazed are masked as well.

Doorways are carefully covered so that the chemicals can’t escape the room. Also, this prevents dust from getting into the room.

Reglazing the Fixture

Our expert will apply 2 coats of primer (we use only top quality products).

2 or 3 layers of glaze are sprayed over the entire surface of the tub. The coating is a two-part coating solution based on urethane.

If necessary, our expert re-caulks the bathtub. That’s it. Now the glaze needs to cure for around 48 hours.

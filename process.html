6 Steps to Refinish Your Bathtub

We use the best primers and glazing solutions to deliver outstanding quality to each and every one of our clients. Our experts are very well trained and equipped and we use only the best paints and solutions to deliver outstanding customer service – each and every time. We are the best in the Houston area when it comes to refinishing, reglazing and resurfacing.

1

Our expert removes all the old paint from the tub.*

2

We thoroughly clean the bathtub and remove the loose caulking.

3

We sand down the surface to remove all the remaining particles and dust.

4

We carefully wash the entire surface of the tub to ensure a perfect bond with the paint

5

We repair all the chips and clean the rust – if necessary

6

We wipe the tub thoroughly before starting the reglazing process.

*If your bathtub has been reglazed previously, please notify our expert. We can strip the old glaze and refinish your tub from scratch. You will have to pay an additional fee, unless we can sand down the tub and apply the reglazing solution over the existing glaze.

Masking the Room Before Spraying:

Everything surrounding the tub is masked with masking paper, to avoid overspray.

All the fixtures are carefully covered by our expert to protect them.

All doorways are covered so that dust can't get into the work area and paint can't get out of the room.

Reglazing the Surface

The expert sprays the tub with 2 coats of high quality primer.

The surface of the tub is then sprayed with 2 or 3 coats of glaze, a 2-part epoxy, urethane-based solution.

The tub is re-caulked, if necessary.
